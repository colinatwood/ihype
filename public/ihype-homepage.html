The scene only grows if the people who love it say so.

Every major streaming platform is optimized to make shareholders money. The result: the same 40 artists dominating every chart, regional scenes invisible, and ticket prices climbing because the people taking fees have no reason to stop. iHYPE only works if fans use it. The algorithm reads what’s loved locally and surfaces it. The charts rank what’s genuinely hyped — not what’s paid to appear. If fans use it and share it, independent music gets louder. If only big labels know about it, it becomes another platform they can game.

This is the mechanism: your HYPE is a vote that doesn’t expire instantly, doesn’t disappear behind a paywall, and can’t be bought. Share iHYPE with one person who loves music and you’ve added one more uncorrupted voice to a system built around uncorrupted voices.

The 45 / 45 / 10 split

Artist 45%: they made the music. Venue 45%: they hold the room and run the door. Promoter 10%: they brought the audience via their recommendation. No promoter attached? The split rebalances 50/50 to artist and venue.

Why card and tax are shown separately

Most platforms blur Stripe fees and sales tax into a “service fee” that hides their own cut. We itemize them so you can verify — the card fee goes to Stripe, the tax goes to your state. Neither dollar touches iHYPE.

Refunds keep the math intact

If a show cancels, every recipient is debited proportionally — including the platform fee zero. No one keeps a cut they didn’t earn.

Why it’s the best · 03 of 05

Eight things we will never do.

Easier to commit to a list than a vague principle. If iHYPE ever does any of these, you should leave — and this list is baked into the charter that requires a board vote to change.

✕

Charge a platform fee on tickets

The platform fee line is $0 forever. Not “introductory.” Not “during high demand.” Zero.

✕

Gate features behind a subscription

No iHYPE Pro. No Premium. No paywalled charts, analytics, or HYPE multipliers.

✕

Sell or resell user data

Your listening, location, friends, purchases — never packaged, never sold, never shared with brokers.

✕

Accept payment for chart positions

Charts rank on active HYPE only. No label can buy a top spot. Sponsored slots appear at position 4+ and are labelled.

✕

Run non-music ads

No sports betting, crypto, or mortgage refinance. Music-related businesses only, in audio playback only.

✕

Displace top organic recommendations

The top three recommendation slots are always earned. Sponsored content appears at position 4 or later.

✕

Play ads outside music playback

No banners. No interstitials. No pre-rolls on pages. Audio ads only, in the player only.

✕

Take a cut from artist revenue

Tip jars, merch, supporter funds — 100% goes to the artist after card and tax. Full stop.
