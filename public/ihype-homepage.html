02
Reason

Why now is
the moment.

Streaming pays pennies. Ticketing platforms take 25% off the top. Discovery feeds are sold to whoever bids highest. The infrastructure of independent music was built to extract — not to support.

That infrastructure is aging. The tools to replace it exist. The artists, venues, and fans who want something better are ready. We are not waiting for permission.

03
The Way

How we'll
be different.

Four commitments that are structural, not aspirational. Built into how the platform works — not written into a terms of service nobody reads.

🎟️
Zero platform fee on tickets
Every dollar you spend on a ticket goes directly to the people who made the show.
45% artist · 45% venue · 10% promoter · 0% iHYPE
🔍
Auditable recommendations
Every feed shows its weights. Geography, taste, social signal, momentum — all visible. No paid placement. Ever.
40% taste · 25% geo · 20% social · 15% momentum
🎙️
Stream-only free use
Artists choose which tracks promoters can curate into radio shows. Fans stream. Nobody downloads without permission.
Artists retain full ownership · revocable at any time
🏛️
Community governance
A nine-seat board — two seats each for fans, artists, venues, and promoters, plus one executive director. No VC board seats.
Books published · decisions logged · always
