Why iHYPE is different
🔍

Transparent algorithm

Every recommendation is auditable. Fans see exactly why music surfaces. No black box, no pay-to-play, no hidden ranking factors. Click any result in Discover to see its score breakdown.

💰

Zero platform fee on tickets

The 45/45/10 split — artist, venue, promoter — is set per show. iHYPE takes $0 of ticket revenue. Every dollar goes to the people who ran the event. Published on every show page.

🏛

Community governance

A 9-seat board — artists, venues, fans, promoters, no investor seats. Governance decisions require community supermajority. No VC board seat, no product veto. Codified before launch.
